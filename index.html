What is Bhadanow?

Bhadanow is a digital trucking platform connecting transporters, truck drivers, and delivery businesses. It streamlines logistics, ensures fast booking, and brings transparency for all stakeholders.

Why Bhadanow is the Best?

  • Fast, reliable, and transparent trucking network
  • Support for truck drivers with dignity and security
  • Real-time tracking and instant payments
  • Digital-first transport management

Founder Story

Vivek Dohare — a truck driver's son from Gwalior — founded Bhadanow to empower transporters and drivers who are often neglected. His personal journey brings deep understanding to solve real logistics problems.

Vision 2025–2030

By 2030, Bhadanow aims to reach every corner of India — connecting lakhs of drivers and transport businesses with safe, efficient, and digital trucking. We're here to bring respect and recognition to every mile driven.
